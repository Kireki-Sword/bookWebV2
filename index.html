What Inkwell does

A journal for everything you read

Inkwell is built for readers who want more than a simple list. It helps you remember what you read, what you loved, and what moments mattered.

Organize Your Library

Track Reading, Completed, Plan to Read, and Dropped titles.

Write Personal Notes

Save reviews, theories, thoughts, and reactions for every title.

Save Favourite Quotes

Keep meaningful lines with chapter, page, or volume information.

Save Favourite Moments

Save scenes, panels, descriptions, and images that stood out.

Score Everything

Give every title your own personal score from 1 to 10.
